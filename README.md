        <h2 align="center">Client Background</h2>
        <body>
          <strong>TechPub</strong> is a New Zealand e-commerce retailer offering a wide range of consumer electronics and accessories to customers worldwide. Since its launch in 2018, the business has expanded steadily while navigating stronger market competition and adapting to the shifting demands and conditions that emerged during the COVID-19 pandemic. <br><br>
          <strong>TechPub</strong> currently serves over <strong>95,000</strong> customers and has processed more than <strong>120,000</strong> transactions, generating total sales revenue in excess of <strong>$32 million</strong>. The company’s e-commerce dataset captures a range of performance dimensions, including sales, product categories, regional activity, and loyalty programme engagement. <br><br>
Reporting to the Head of Operations, a detailed analysis was undertaken to evaluate <strong>TechPub’s</strong> performance across the 2019–2022 period. The insights from this review support internal teams in refining processes, improving efficiency, and strengthening <strong>TechPub’s</strong> overall commercial performance. The key insights and recommendations focus on the following areas:

<h3>Northstar Metrics</h3> <h4> <ul> <li><strong>Sales trends</strong> – Reviewing core metrics such as total revenue, number of orders, and average order value (AOV).</li> <li><strong>Product performance</strong> – Analysing product category success, refund rates, and overall contribution to sales to inform strategic planning.</li> <li><strong>Loyalty programme analysis</strong> – Assessing the programme’s effectiveness and identifying opportunities to increase customer engagement and retention.</li> <li><strong>Regional performance</strong> – Evaluating regional sales patterns and product demand to highlight areas for growth and operational improvement.</li> </ul> </h4>
